# PS Trade — Research & Design Brief

> Research conducted February 2026 to inform the design of the Executive Command Centre module for the Permanent Secretary for Trade.

## 1. Core Mandate of PS Trade (Kenya & East Africa)

### Structural Position
The Principal Secretary (PS) is the chief accounting officer and administrative head of a State Department. Appointed under Article 155 of the Constitution, they are the highest-ranking civil servant in the department.

### Key Responsibilities
- **Policy Implementation**: Translating Cabinet-level trade policy into operational programs
- **Accounting Officer**: Personally accountable for all expenditure, signs off on budgets and procurement
- **Trade Negotiations**: Oversees Kenya's participation in bilateral/multilateral negotiations (WTO, AfCFTA, EAC, COMESA)
- **Trade Facilitation**: Drives WTO TFA implementation, coordinates with KRA, KEBS, KEPHIS, and port authorities
- **Export Promotion**: Oversees KEPROBA, monitors export targets, coordinates trade missions
- **Trade Licensing & Regulation**: Import/export licensing, trade remedies, compliance
- **Intergovernmental Coordination**: County Trade offices, other ministries, state agencies
- **Parliamentary Reporting**: Responses to parliamentary questions, committee appearances

### Daily Data Needs
- Customs revenue collection vs. targets
- Trade balance snapshot (imports vs. exports)
- Status of pending trade agreements/negotiations
- Port clearance times and trade facilitation metrics
- FX reserves impact from trade flows
- Alerts on trade disputes or NTB complaints
- Export sector performance by commodity and destination

## 2. Key Metrics Monitored

### Tier 1 — Daily/Weekly
| Metric | Description | Source |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Trade Balance | Total exports minus imports (monthly rolling) | Customs/Central Bank |
| Customs Revenue | Duty + VAT collected vs. budget target | KRA |
| Export Volume/Value | By top 10 commodities | KRA/KNBS |
| Import Volume/Value | By category (petroleum, machinery, food) | KRA/KNBS |
| Port Dwell Time | Average clearance time at Mombasa/Nairobi ICD | KPA/KRA |
| Trade Facilitation Index | Time-to-clear, documents, cost-to-export/import | World Bank |
| FX Rate Impact | KES vs USD/EUR/CNY competitiveness | Central Bank |

### Tier 2 — Monthly/Quarterly Strategic
- Balance of Payments (trade component)
- Export Diversification Index
- Terms of Trade (export price / import price)
- Regional trade share (EAC, COMESA, AfCFTA vs. rest of world)
- Non-Tariff Barrier cases (EAC/COMESA mechanisms)
- Trade agreement utilization rate
- Trade deficit by partner (China, India, UAE, EU, US)
- SME export participation

### Tier 3 — Annual/Strategic
- WTO TFA Implementation Score (Category A/B/C)
- AfCFTA tariff liberalization progress
- Doing Business / Trade Across Borders rank
- Export growth rate (YoY)
- Import substitution progress
- FDI inflows to trade sectors

## 3. Global Benchmarks — Trade Ministry Digital Systems

### Singapore (TradeNet / Networked Trade Platform)
- World gold standard, connects 35+ agencies
- Real-time trade flow visualization, predictive analytics
- Automated customs risk scoring, end-to-end supply chain visibility
- Single trade document submission, automated permit approvals (under 10 minutes)
- Blockchain-based certificate of origin verification

### UK (Department for Business and Trade)
- Trade Data Dashboard (public and internal executive versions)
- Trade negotiation pipeline tracker (20+ countries)
- Export support program KPIs, inward investment pipeline
- Chief Data Officer function providing ministerial briefings

### US (Department of Commerce / ITA)
- TradeStats Express and Trade Policy Information System
- Country Commercial Guides per market
- Tariff/FTA utilization analytics
- Section 301/232 monitoring dashboards

### EU (DG Trade)
- Market Access Database (tariffs, barriers, defense measures)
- FTA implementation scorecards for all EU agreements
- Access2Markets Portal (product-specific trade information)

### Common Elements Across All Systems
1. Single-window data integration
2. Real-time or near-real-time data
3. Predictive analytics
4. Alert systems for threshold breaches
5. Geospatial visualization (trade corridors, port heat maps)
6. Drill-down capability (national aggregate to HS code level)

## 4. African Context

### AfCFTA Implementation Tracking
- Tariff offer schedules by member states
- Rules of origin negotiations by sector
- Services liberalization commitments
- Dispute settlement and trade remedy cases
- Intra-African trade as % of total (target: 15% to 25%+)

### Regional Trade Blocs
| Bloc | Key Metrics |
|------|-------------|
| EAC | Intra-EAC trade, CET compliance, Single Customs Territory, NTB tracker, OSBP efficiency |
| COMESA | Preferential trade utilization, FTA benefit analysis, Tripartite FTA progress |
| SADC | Cross-membership trade (Kenya not a member but trades with SADC states) |
| ECOWAS | Growing corridor especially Nigeria-Kenya via AfCFTA |

### Key Bilateral Agreements
- Kenya-US: STIP progress
- Kenya-EU: EPA implementation
- Kenya-UK: Economic Partnership Agreement
- Kenya-UAE/Gulf: Trade volumes and investment flows
- Kenya-China: Trade deficit monitoring (largest bilateral deficit)

### Trade Infrastructure
- Northern Corridor (Mombasa-Nairobi-Kampala-Kigali) transit monitoring
- LAPSSET Corridor development
- Standard Gauge Railway cargo utilization
- Port of Mombasa throughput and efficiency

## 5. Authority-Level Features Implemented

### Executive Briefing System
- Morning Brief concept (auto-generated daily digest)
- Weekly Strategic Summary (charts for Cabinet Secretary)
- Parliamentary Q&A prep database

### Policy Impact Simulation (Future)
- "What-if" tariff scenarios
- Revenue impact calculator for trade concessions
- Export growth projections under policy scenarios

### Alert System (Implemented)
- Critical: Trade balance deterioration, revenue shortfall, dispute escalation
- High: Approaching NTB deadlines, negotiation milestones, FX volatility
- Medium: Revenue surplus notifications, positive milestones
- Info: WTO reviews, routine updates

### Visual Authority Markers (Implemented)
- Republic of Kenya crest/seal
- "Office of the Principal Secretary" designation
- "Classified — For Official Use Only" footer
- Deep navy palette distinct from standard app theme
- Gold gradient accents with government authority feel
