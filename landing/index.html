The Future

CBDC-powered
trade finance

Smart Trade Africa is built to integrate Central Bank Digital Currencies, unlocking new financial models for the African trade ecosystem.

🔄 Dynamic Discounting

Buyers offer early payment discounts to suppliers via CBDC for prompt settlement, optimizing cash flow and reducing financing costs.

🏢 Reverse Factoring

Buyers arrange CBDC-based financing for suppliers, leveraging stronger credit ratings for favourable terms and efficient fund disbursement.

📄 Invoice Financing

Tokenize invoices on blockchain as collateral for short-term financing, providing transparency and reducing administrative overhead.

📦 Supply Chain Tokenization

Represent supply chain assets as digital tokens for trading and collateral, fostering liquidity and creating new investment opportunities.

🏆 Performance-based Financing

Smart contracts automate payment releases based on predefined milestones, incentivizing suppliers to meet quality benchmarks.

🤝 Peer-to-Peer Lending

Suppliers access financing directly from investors within the network, bypassing traditional intermediaries with seamless CBDC settlement.
